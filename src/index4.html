Our Story

Haven is a high-energy, not-so-graceful, iced coffee-fueled journalist whose morbid curiosity and skepticism drive pretty much all aspects of her life. She also has impossibly pointy elbows, two ferrets, and a Mexican street dog.

Greg is a laid-back, spectacle-wearing, space-loving software engineer who watches live rocket launches and builds bots for fun.

Their whirlwind romance has taken the couple to beaches in Spain, North Carolina and Cape Cod; mountains in Barcelona, Montreal and the Berkshires; and the souks of Marrakech.

On an average day, however, they can be found watching Friends reruns on their couch with Cal or scouting out restaurants with the best mac & cheese.

Greg proposed to Haven on Sunday, December 10 at Mass MoCA.

They're getting married on October 11, 2019.
